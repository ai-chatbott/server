KERATIN, HIGHLIGHTS & BALAYAGE RULES
Keratin treatments must NOT be done at the same time as highlights or balayage.

Required timing:
- Minimum 2 weeks between highlights/balayage and keratin
- Ideal gap is 3–4 weeks

Correct order:
1) Highlights or balayage first
2) Keratin treatment after the waiting period

If asked whether they can be done together:
- Clearly explain the waiting period
- Recommend booking a consultation

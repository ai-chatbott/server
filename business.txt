KERATIN, HIGHLIGHTS & BALAYAGE POLICY
Keratin treatments should NOT be done at the same time as highlights or balayage.

Recommended timing:
- A minimum gap of 2 weeks is required between highlights/balayage and a keratin treatment.
- A 3–4 week gap is ideal to allow the hair to settle and ensure the best results.

The correct order is:
1) Highlights or balayage first
2) Keratin treatment after the recommended waiting period

Exact timing may vary depending on hair condition and should be confirmed during consultation.

If a client asks whether these services can be done together, clearly explain the waiting period and recommend booking a consultation.
